Roadmap

Roadmap for Signs & Symbols Coin +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Phase 1: Concept and Foundation | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ Q1 2024: • Official Launch the Signs & Symbols Coin token on pump.fun • Begin community building on social media platforms +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Phase 2: Early Development and Community Growth | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ Q2 2024: • Release the interactive symbol archive, allowing users to explore and search historical symbols • Implement basic symbol creation tools • Begin symbol upload functionality for community contributions • Integrate user profiles for symbol ownership and contribution tracking +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Phase 3: Symbol Marketplace & NFT Integration | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ Q3 2024: • Launch NFT minting for symbol creation, allowing users to tokenize their designs • Open a marketplace where users can buy, sell, and trade tokenized symbols • Introduce community-driven challenges and rewards (e.g., symbol creation contests) • Develop symbol analytics features, including historical data and trends • Implement symbol-based NFTs with proof of ownership and rarity +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Phase 4: Advanced Features & Partnerships | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ Q4 2024: • Launch AR symbol explorer, allowing users to interact with symbols in real-world settings • Develop partnerships with NFT platforms, art galleries, and metaverse projects for symbol integration • Expand cultural and historical resources on the platform to provide deeper insights into each symbol's origin and meaning • Implement multilingual support to make the platform accessible worldwide • Enhance gamification elements, allowing users to collect, trade, and complete symbol-based quests +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Phase 5: Community Engagement & Global Expansion | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ 2025: • Introduce decentralized governance for the platform, allowing the community to vote on updates, symbol categorization, and new features • Roll out mobile app for easier access and symbol management • Continue community-driven events to encourage symbol creation and sharing • Launch educational initiatives like webinars, workshops, and partnerships with schools or universities to promote the project globally • Expand global partnerships and integrations with cultural, historical, and creative organizations +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ | Future Vision | +------------------------------------------------------------------------------+ Establish Signs & Symbols Coin as the premier platform for symbol discovery, creation, and exchange in both the physical and digital worlds, blending history with the future of blockchain technology.
